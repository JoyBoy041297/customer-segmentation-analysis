# Customer Segmentation \& Revenue Analysis

## Overview

This project analyzes over 500,000 e-commerce transactions to understand customer behavior, revenue distribution, and business performance.

## Tools Used

* Python (Pandas)
* Matplotlib \& Seaborn
* Jupyter Notebook

## Key Analysis

* Data cleaning and preprocessing
* Revenue calculation (Quantity × UnitPrice)
* Customer segmentation (High / Medium / Low value)
* Country-wise revenue analysis
* Monthly sales trend analysis

## Key Insights

* Majority of customers are low-value, while a small segment contributes most revenue
* Customer 14646 is the highest revenue-generating customer
* United Kingdom is the top-performing market
* Sales increase toward the end of the year, indicating seasonal demand

## Business Recommendations

* Retain high-value customers through loyalty programs
* Upsell medium-value customers
* Improve engagement for low-value customers through targeted campaigns
